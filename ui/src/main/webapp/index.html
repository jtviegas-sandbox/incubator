Welcome to IEM Automation Plan Action Analyser

Problem Statement

What is an IEM Automation Plan Action (APA)?

IEM uses APAs to remotely manage and deliver software updates to its clients (also known as endpoints). IEM users currently have no way of finding out why certain APAs failed or why they took so long to complete.

Motivation for Project

Give Endpoint Managers a tool to analyze and evaluate bottlenecks and performance issues impacting APAs by leveraging Analytics Services on the BlueMix platform including PI Infostream Streams.

Use existing technologies and services on the IBM BleMix platform to present Automation Plan Action reports in a visual and easily readable/ understandable way.
